About Me

I'm a software developer born and raised in the Portland, Oregon metropolitan area. What I enjoy most about software development is encountering a unique problem, dissecting that problem, and solving it. I don't let obstacles get in my way of solving something. I firmly believe that the way you approach a problem is important, and I personally like to break a problem up into steps in order to solve it. There's a great sense of reward when doing this, and there's a lot of satisfaction in coming up with a solution that someone else may not have seen. I have recentely graduated from The Tech Academy of Portland's Software Development boot camp. This was an intensive program with nearly 1000 hours of instruction and hands-on training in full-stack software development. I was trained how to utilize many different languages and tools such as HTML5/CSS3, Javascript and jQuery, SQL, Python, C#, .NET framework, and Entity Framework. The program also familiarzed me with Git, Azure, and Agile/Scrum methodology. I am confident in my abilities, and I am confident in my ability to quicky learn new things. I love learning and the tech industry is a field full of different roles and tools. Whenever I encounter something that I don't quite understand, or don't know, I make it my mission to understand it. I am looking for the opportunity to apply my knowledge and continue to grow as a developer.
